What is ForumCompass?

ForumCompass is a political compass test that measures 3 axes of political ideals within the Caiti Forums. Those 3 axes are Authority vs. Liberty, Government intervention in activity, and Progressivism vs. Conservatism. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a Forum political compass.

* The questions were written entirely by me, and I had to think of the same amount of questions as the original test, so some may be somewhat dumb.
